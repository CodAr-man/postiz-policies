Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

At OceanVault (self-hosted Postiz instance at oceanvault.store), we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

  • Data required to connect your social media accounts (via official APIs such as Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, etc.).
  • Scheduling and publishing information you provide (e.g., captions, media files, post times).

How We Use Information

We use the information you provide only for the purpose of scheduling and publishing posts to your connected accounts. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Data Storage

All data is securely stored on our self-hosted server. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Cookies

Our site may use basic cookies to maintain your login session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing us.
